🔐 Connect Your YouTube Music Account

How to get your headers

  1. Open YouTube Music and log in
  2. Press F12 to open DevTools
  3. Click the Network tab
  4. Play any song or click anything on the page(eg. Library)
  5. Look for a request to music.youtube.com
  6. if not type browse
  7. Click it → scroll to Request Headers
  8. Copy everything in that section and paste below
