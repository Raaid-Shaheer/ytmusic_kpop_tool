How to get your headers

1
Open YouTube Music and make sure you're logged in
2
Press F12 to open DevTools → click the Network tab
3
Click anything on the page (e.g. Library). In the filter bar type browse
4
Click any browse request → scroll to Request Headers
5
Right-click the headers section → Copy all → paste below
